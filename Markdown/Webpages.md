# **Web Application Content Outline**

---

## **1. Homepage**

### **Content:**
- **Header:**
  - Logo and restaurant name.
  - Navigation menu (Login/Signup/Account, Toggler{ Home, About, Menu, Reservations, Contact }).
- **Hero Section:**
  - High-quality image or video of the restaurant.
  - Tagline or welcome message.
  - Call-to-action buttons.
- **Featured Section:**
  - Highlight special dishes or promotions (with images and short descriptions).
  - Link to the full menu.
- **About Us Preview:**
  - Brief introduction to the restaurant .
  - Link to the full About page.
- **Testimonials:**
  - Customer reviews with star ratings and photos.
- **Footer:**
  - About summary.
  - Social media icons.
  - Contact information (address, phone, email).
  - Feedback form.

---

## **2. About Page**

### **Content:**
- **Header:** Same as homepage.
- **Our Story:**
  - History of the restaurant.
  - Mission and values.
- **Meet the Team:**
  - Photos and bios of key staff.
- **Awards & Recognition:**
  - Display any awards or certifications.
- **Gallery:**
  - Photos of the restaurant, dishes, and events.
- **Footer:** Same as homepage.

---

## **3. Menu Page**

### **Content:**
- **Header:** Same as homepage.
- **Menu Categories:**
  - Sections for different meal types (e.g., Starters, Mains, Desserts, Beverages).
- **Menu Items:**
  - Each item should include:
    - Name.
    - Description.
    - Price.
    - Image.
    - Dietary icons (e.g., vegan, gluten-free).
- **Search/Filter Options:**
  - Filter by category, dietary preferences, or price range.
- **Call-to-Action:**
  - "Order Now" button for each item (links to the ordering system).
- **Footer:** Same as homepage.

---

## **4. Reservation Page**

### **Content:**
- **Header:** Same as homepage.
- **Reservation Form:**
  - Fields for:
    - Name.
    - Email.
    - Phone number.
    - Date and time.
    - Number of guests.
    - Special requests (e.g., dietary needs, occasion).
- **Table Availability:**
  - Show available time slots in real-time.
- **Confirmation:**
  - Display a confirmation message after booking.
- **Footer:** Same as homepage.

## **6. Admin Page**

### **Content:**
- **Login Section:**
  - Secure login for admins.
- **Dashboard:**
  - Overview of key metrics (e.g., total reservations, orders, revenue).
- **Manage Menu:**
  - Add, edit, or delete menu items.
- **Manage Reservations:**
  - View, confirm, or cancel reservations.
- **Manage Orders:**
  - View and update order statuses.
- **User Management:**
  - View and manage customer and staff accounts.
- **Notifications:**
  - Send promotions or alerts to users.
- **Reports:**
  - Generate reports on sales, reservations, and customer feedback.

## **7. User Dashboard**

### **Content:**
- **Login/Signup Section:**

- **Profile Management:**

## **8. Additional Pages**

### **8.1 Events Page**

#### **Content:**
- **Header:** Same as homepage.
- **Upcoming Events:**
  - List of events with:
    - Event name.
    - Date and time.
    - Description.
    - Image or banner.

- **Footer:** Same as homepage.

---

### **8.2 FAQ Page**
The FAQ page addresses common questions and concerns from customers.

#### **Content:**
- **Header:** Same as homepage.
- **Frequently Asked Questions:**
  - Organized into categories (e.g., Reservations, Orders, Payments, General).
  - Example questions:
    - "How do I make a reservation?"
    - "Can I modify or cancel my order?"
    - "What payment methods do you accept?"
    - "Do you offer vegetarian or gluten-free options?"
- **Search Bar:**
  - Allow users to search for specific questions.
- **Contact Support:**
  - Link to the Contact page or a support form for further assistance.
- **Footer:** Same as homepage.

---

### **8.3 Privacy Policy & Terms of Service Page**
This page provides legal information to users, ensuring transparency and compliance.

#### **Content:**
- **Header:** Same as homepage.
- **Privacy Policy:**
  - Explain how user data is collected, stored, and used.
  - Include information on cookies, third-party services, and data protection.
- **Terms of Service:**
  - Outline the rules and guidelines for using the website.
  - Include disclaimers, liability clauses, and user responsibilities.
- **Footer:** Same as homepage.
